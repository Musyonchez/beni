# Chapter 1: Introduction

**Target Word Count**: ~2,500 words  
**Target Pages**: 4 pages

## Structure

### 1.1 History (~800 words)
**Content to include:**
- Background of the problem domain
- Evolution of current solutions
- Historical context of your project area
- Why traditional methods are inadequate
- Emergence of digital solutions

**Writing approach:**
- Start broad, then narrow to your specific area
- Use citations from literature
- Establish the need for innovation

---

### 1.2 Problem Statement (~1,200 words)
**Content to include:**
- Specific problem your project addresses
- Who is affected (target users)
- Current pain points and challenges
- Consequences of the problem
- Why existing solutions are insufficient
- Scope of the problem

**Writing approach:**
- Be specific and concrete
- Use data/statistics if available
- Include real-world examples
- Clearly articulate the gap you're filling

---

### 1.3 Conclusion (~500 words)
**Content to include:**
- Summary of the chapter
- Transition to next chapter
- Restate the core problem
- Brief mention of your proposed solution

**Writing approach:**
- Concise recap
- Forward-looking statements
- Set expectations for what follows
