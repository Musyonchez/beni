### 2.1 Introduction (~400 words)
**File**: `2.1-introduction.md`
- Purpose of literature review
- Scope (global, regional, local)

### 2.2 Global Perspective (~3,500 words)
**File**: `2.2-global-perspective.md`
- Review 2-3 international systems
- Each system: ~1,200 words + screenshots
- Examples: Grubhub, Uber Eats, campus apps from US/Europe

### 2.3 Regional Perspective (~3,000 words)
**File**: `2.3-regional-perspective.md`
- Review 2 African systems
- Each: ~1,500 words + screenshots

### 2.4 Local Perspective (~2,100 words)
**File**: `2.4-local-perspective.md`

- Each: ~1,000-1,100 words

### 2.5 Strengths and Weaknesses (~1,200 words)

- Gaps your project will fill

**File**: `2.6-conclusion.md`
- Summary of findings
- Justification for your project
