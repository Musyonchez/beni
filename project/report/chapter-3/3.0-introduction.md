# 3.0 Introduction

This chapter outlines the research design and methodology adopted in the development and evaluation of FarmLink — an online agricultural produce eMarketplace for Kenyan smallholder farmers. It describes the locality and intended beneficiaries of the project, the research design approach, the target population and sampling method, the data collection methods employed, the data analysis techniques applied, the system testing plan, and the ethical considerations observed throughout the study. The methodology presented in this chapter guided both the investigative research conducted to understand the needs of the target users and the technical development of the FarmLink prototype.
