# 2.0 Introduction

This chapter presents a review of existing literature and systems relevant to the development of FarmLink — an online agricultural produce eMarketplace for Kenyan smallholder farmers. The chapter is structured into three main parts. The first part analyses three existing agricultural eMarketplace systems from different countries, comparing their features, strengths, and weaknesses in relation to the proposed system. The second part reviews literature directly related to the two specific research objectives of this study: the challenges associated with traditional and intermediary-based agricultural market systems, and the benefits that digital eMarketplace platforms offer to smallholder farmers. The third part presents a conclusion that synthesises the reviewed literature and establishes the gap that FarmLink seeks to address.
