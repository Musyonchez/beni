# Agricultural E-Commerce Platform — Questionnaire (Farmers)

This questionnaire aims to gather insights on agricultural trade practices and the challenges faced by farmers in accessing markets. The collected information will contribute to the development of an agricultural e-commerce platform designed to connect farmers directly with buyers. Responses are confidential and will only be used for academic purposes.
