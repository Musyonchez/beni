# Questionnaire Design Guide

Use this as a reference whenever a new questionnaire needs to be created for this project.

---

## Structure Rules

- **Total questions: 9**
- **All questions must be closed-ended** (multiple choice or checkbox — no open-ended text answers)

---

## Section Breakdown

### Questions 1–3 — Respondent Bio Data
Capture who the respondent is. Examples:
- Age bracket
- Gender
- Role / category (farmer, extension officer, researcher, etc.)

### Questions 4–6 — Knowledge, Skills & Experience
Assess the respondent's familiarity with the topic. Examples:
- Current practices / how they do things today
- Frequency of technology use
- Years of experience in the field

### Questions 7–8 — Objectives Testing (Checkbox)
Directly test the research objectives from **Chapter 2, Sections 2.2.1 and 2.2.2**.
- Q7 tests the **problem/challenges** objective — what difficulties respondents face with current methods
- Q8 tests the **solution/benefits** objective — what features or outcomes they value in the proposed system
- Both must be **checkbox questions** (tick all that apply)

### Question 9 — Recommendation
Single closing question:
> "Would you recommend [the proposed system] to others in your community?"
- Yes
- Maybe
- No

---

## Approval Process

- Do **not** share the questionnaire before supervisor approval
- Submit for review and wait for explicit sign-off before distribution
