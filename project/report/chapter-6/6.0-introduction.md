# 6.0 Introduction

This chapter presents the discussion, conclusion, recommendations, and proposed future work arising from the research and system development conducted for FarmLink. The discussion section interprets the findings presented in Chapter Five in relation to the literature reviewed in Chapter Two, examining the extent to which the empirical data corroborates or extends existing knowledge on agricultural market challenges and the value of digital eMarketplace solutions. The conclusion addresses both specific research objectives directly and confirms the relevance of the system developed. Recommendations are provided for three stakeholder groups — farmers, government, and future developers — and the chapter closes with proposed directions for future enhancement of the FarmLink platform beyond the scope of this project.
