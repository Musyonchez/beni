# 5.0 Introduction

This chapter presents the findings of the primary research conducted in support of the two specific research objectives of this study. Data were collected through structured questionnaires administered to smallholder farmers and buyers in the peri-urban areas of Limuru, Kikuyu, and Kiambu, as described in Chapter Three. The chapter is organised into four sections: the response rate, the demographic profile of respondents, findings related to Specific Research Objective One (SRO1) on the challenges of traditional and intermediary-based market systems, and findings related to Specific Research Objective Two (SRO2) on the perceived benefits of adopting an online agricultural produce eMarketplace. All quantitative data are presented as frequencies and percentages and are supported by bar charts and tables where appropriate. Qualitative observations gathered during informal interviews are used to contextualise and enrich the quantitative findings.
