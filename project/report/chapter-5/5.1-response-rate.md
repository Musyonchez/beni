The sample comprised 20 smallholder farmers and 20 buyers of locally produced agricultural goods, as determined by the sampling strategy outlined in Chapter Three. Mugenda and Mugenda (2003) suggest that a response rate of 70 percent or above is acceptable for social science research, and the rate achieved in this study falls within that threshold. The relatively high rate of return is attributed to the direct administration approach used by the researcher, in which questionnaires were distributed and collected in person at Wakulima Market and within farming cooperatives in Kiambu County, allowing the researcher to clarify items and encourage completion on the spot.
