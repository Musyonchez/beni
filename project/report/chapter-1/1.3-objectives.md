# 1.3 Project Objectives

## 1.3.1 Overall Goal

To design and develop FarmLink, an online agricultural produce eMarketplace for Kenyan smallholder farmers that enables direct transactions between farmers and buyers through a secure and accessible mobile application.

## 1.3.2 Systems Design and Development Objectives

The following objectives define the unique technical features to be designed and developed as part of the FarmLink system:

i. To implement a geolocation module that enables buyers to discover farmers and available produce within their geographical proximity, reducing unnecessary travel and improving the efficiency of the farm-to-buyer connection.

ii. To develop a shopping cart and M-Pesa payment integration that allows buyers to select multiple produce items and complete transactions securely within the application, without the need for cash or third-party payment channels.

iii. To build a real-time order tracking feature that enables buyers to monitor the status of their orders — from placement through confirmation, dispatch, and delivery — providing transparency and accountability throughout the fulfilment process.

iv. To create a seller rating and review system that allows buyers to evaluate farmers and the quality of produce received, promoting a culture of quality assurance and building trust between buyers and sellers on the platform.

## 1.3.3 Specific Research Objectives

**1.3.3.1** To identify the traditional challenges faced by Kenyan smallholder farmers when using manual and intermediary-based market systems.

**1.3.3.2** To determine the benefits of adopting an online agricultural produce eMarketplace for Kenyan smallholder farmers.
