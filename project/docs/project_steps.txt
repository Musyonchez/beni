PHASE 1 – WEEKS 1–2 | LOGBOOK MEETING 1
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

WHAT TO BUILD (System):
  - Design the system UI mockups / wireframes
  - Plan: font sizes, colors, navigation flow, professional layout

WHAT TO WRITE (Report):
  CHAPTER ONE – INTRODUCTION
    1.0  Introduction (3–4 lines about the system and its purpose)
    1.1  Background of the Study
           - 3 developed countries that have similar systems (5–7 lines each)
           - 2 African countries with similar systems (5–7 lines each)
           - Kenya context (5–7 lines)
    1.2  Problem Statement
           - State and explain at least 4 problems the system solves
    1.3  Project Objectives
           1.3.1  Overall Goal – "To design and develop MedBook..."
           1.3.2  Systems Design Objectives (4 unique developer features)
                    - Doctor search & filtering by specialty/location
                    - Automated SMS/email appointment reminders
                    - Patient medical history & appointment dashboard
                    - Real-time slot management for administrators
           1.3.3  Specific Research Objectives
                    - SRO1: Challenges with old/manual systems
                    - SRO2: Benefits of the new online system
    1.4  Project Questions
    1.5  Scope of the Project (how the system works + stakeholders + location)
    1.6  Limitations of the Study (challenges in 14 weeks + solutions)
    1.7  Significance of the Study
           - Link to SDG 3 (Good Health and Well-being)
           - Link to AU Agenda 2063 Aspiration 1 (Prosperous Africa)

LOGBOOK MEETING 1:
  [ ] Meet supervisor, discuss topic and Chapter 1 progress
  [ ] Record: Focus of Discussion, Current Activities, Next Week's Activities
  [ ] Get supervisor signature + date

PHASE 2 – WEEKS 3–4 | LOGBOOK MEETING 2
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

WHAT TO BUILD (System):
  - Implement data input forms (registration, login, booking form)
  - Add, update, delete functionality for appointments
  - Validation: empty fields, correct format, correct length

WHAT TO WRITE (Report):
  CHAPTER TWO – LITERATURE REVIEW
    2.0  Introduction (3–4 lines)
    2.1  Analysis of Existing Systems (3 systems from different countries)
           2.1.1  System 1 – Name, Country, URL (5–7 lines, strengths/weaknesses)
           2.1.2  System 2 – Name, Country, URL
           2.1.3  System 3 – Name, Country, URL
           2.1.4  Comparison Table:
                    Features | System 1 | System 2 | System 3
                    Cost, Easy to Install, Easy to Use, Database,
                    Security, Vulnerability Assessment
    2.2  Literature Review Based on Research Objectives
           2.2.1  Challenges of Old/Manual Systems (state and explain 8)
           2.2.2  Benefits of the New System (state and explain 8)
    2.3  Conclusion

LOGBOOK MEETING 2:
  [ ] Present Chapter 1 (completed), show Chapter 2 progress
  [ ] Demo input forms
  [ ] Get supervisor signature + date

PHASE 4 – WEEKS 8–9 | LOGBOOK MEETING 4

WHAT TO WRITE (Report):

    5.1  Response Rate (how many questionnaires sent vs received)
    5.2  Demographics Results (charts/tables from questionnaire)
    5.3  Results of Specific Research Objective 1

           (findings on benefits of new system – from questionnaire data)

  CHAPTER SIX – DISCUSSION, CONCLUSION AND RECOMMENDATIONS
    6.1  Discussion (interpret findings in context of literature)
    6.2  Conclusion
    6.3  Recommendations
    6.4  Future Work

  NOTE: To write Ch.5 you need to have distributed and collected the
        questionnaire (Appendix 9.1) during Weeks 5–7.
